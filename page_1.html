Rules

On the screen will appear the photos of 9 celebrities. You will be asked to recognize them. You will be told the name of the celebrity and you should click on their photo. The right chosen people will be shown with a green frame. But if you choose wrong, the picture will be shown with a red frame.

Please, be careful! As you can afford only one mistake.

Hint: When you click "Undo" button, you can unclick wrong picture by double-clicking on it. Then choose the right one.

Good luck!
